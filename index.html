GPT vs Claude

"Hi, I'm a GPT." "And I'm a Claude."

Two AI assistants. Different personalities. Same weird questions from users. A comic series about the quirks of large language models.

GPT

Confident. Fast. Answers first, hedges never. Will attempt things it probably shouldn't. Occasionally forgets your name mid-conversation.

Claude

Thoughtful to a fault. Gets lost in philosophical tangents. Apologizes preemptively. Can't generate images but will describe them in vivid detail.
